State-Wise Migration Trends in Malaysia

The state-wise map highlights that Selangor, Kuala Lumpur, and Johor are the top destinations for migrants during this period. These economically advanced states attract a significant portion of foreign labor, particularly in urban and industrial centers. In contrast, less industrialized states in East Malaysia saw lower levels of migration.

Gender Breakdown of Migrants by Country (Top 10 Countries)

The gender breakdown bar chart reveals the male and female migration distribution from the top 10 countries. Countries like Indonesia and the Philippines send a more balanced mix of male and female migrants, while countries like Bangladesh and Pakistan primarily send male migrants. This reflects the types of jobs that migrants take up in Malaysia, with men often in construction and women in caregiving roles.
